The simple mail client

## Why

We wanted to make an email client that felt more natural to use for developers and power users alike: an email client that worked the way you would want emails to work!

## What we use?

- [React](https://react.dev/)
- [React Native](https://reactnative.dev)
- [Next.js](https://nextjs.org) (web application)
- [Ink](https://term.ink/) (tui)
- [Tauri](https://tauri.app) (desktop)
- [Convex](https://www.convex.dev) (backend)
